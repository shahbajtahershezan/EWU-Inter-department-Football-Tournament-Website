Tournament Rules

Official guidelines for the knockout stage matches

Match Regulations

  • Two halves of 35 minutes each
  • 15 minutes halftime break
  • Injury time at referee's discretion

  • Maximum of 5 substitutions per match
  • Substitutions at any stoppage
  • Substituted players cannot return

  • 15 minutes extra time if tied
  • Penalty shootout if still tied
  • Best of 5, then sudden death
Disciplinary Rules
Yellow Card
Warning - 2 yellows = suspension
Red Card
Ejection + next match suspension
Player Eligibility
Valid EWU ID required
Team Requirements
Minimum 7 players to start
