Tournament Venue

East West University Football Ground

A/2, Jahurul Islam Avenue, Jahurul Islam City, Aftabnagar, Dhaka-1212

Matches will be held from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM on scheduled days

Capacity: 500 spectators

Ample parking space available

Food and beverage stalls will be available

Free entry for all EWU students and staff

Tournament Rules

  • Each match will consist of two halves of 30 minutes each
  • Teams must have at least 7 players to start a match
  • Maximum of 3 substitutions allowed per match
  • Yellow card: warning, Red card: immediate ejection
  • Group stage: 3 points for win, 1 for draw, 0 for loss
  • Top two teams from each group advance to knockout stage
  • In case of a tie in knockout stages, penalty shootout will decide
  • All players must have valid EWU ID cards
  • Referee decisions are final and binding
